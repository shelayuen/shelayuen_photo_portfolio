About Me

Marketing Manager & Digital Designer

Shela is a multi-disciplinary Digital Designer and Digital Marketing Strategist who bridges the worlds of business and design.

With award-winning sales & business development experience in the financial sectors, she brings a rare combination of strategic thinking and visual storytelling to every project.

Her journey spans from managing corporate client relationships and sales channels at Manulife Hong Kong, to earning her Million Dollar Round Table qualification as an insurance advisor, to most recently completing her diploma in New Media Design & Web Development at BCIT. This diverse background allows her to translate complex business objectives into compelling digital experiences that resonate with premium audiences.

Shela's superpower lies in her ability to empathize deeply with users—a skill honed through years of customer-facing roles—while applying analytical rigor from her Master's in Philosophy. Whether designing intuitive interfaces, crafting brand narratives, or developing web solutions, she approaches each challenge with both creative vision and business strategy in mind.

Currently based in Vancouver, Shela specializes in helping luxury brands and premium service providers elevate their digital presence through thoughtful design that drives results.
